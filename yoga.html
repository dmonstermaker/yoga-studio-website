The founder

Rooted in science,
guided by tradition

Dr. Akhilesh Kumar Singh holds a Post Graduate degree in Yoga and brings over 18 years of diagnostic and treatment experience. His approach uniquely bridges classical yogic wisdom with the rigour of modern medical science — particularly his research in Yoga and Psychoneuroimmunology.

At Nirmala Yoga, you are not assigned a routine. You receive a thorough Nadi diagnosis first, and then a fully personalised therapeutic path — one designed to address the root cause of your condition, not just manage symptoms.

The institute has a plan for withdrawal from medication where possible, working in coordination with your treating physician — a rare, medically responsible approach in the yoga world.
